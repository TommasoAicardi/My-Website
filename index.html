About Me

PhD student, Department of Social and Political Science - Bocconi University, Milan, Italy.

Research Interests

I am currently interested in understanding how living with a frail family member shapes the health and life trajectories of other household members. More broadly, I would like to explore themes at the intersection of health demography, family sociology, and social stratification, ideally through computational approaches or a life-course perspective.

Datasets and Resources

  • DOEM-UNIBO A dataset dedicated to the study of political communication, agenda setting, and leadership style in Italy. It comprises transcripts of 2,889 episodes of the Italian political talk show Otto e mezzo (La7), broadcast between April 4, 2011 and June 10, 2025, together with each episode's air date and guest list. Built to support research on media agendas, coverage of major political events (e.g. COVID-19, the invasion of Ukraine), political leaders' communication styles, and public policy sentiment. Created with Chloe Papadopoulou (University of Amsterdam) and Marco Albertini (University of Bologna). Freely available under a CC BY-NC-SA license. Download dataset ↗ Announcement ↗
